Biography

Noah Bowers is a multidisciplinary artist who creates comics because he believes they are a great medium for storytelling and that good stories are integral for humanity’s survival. He hopes to inspire others by drawing cartoons as a career, and is on his way to producing his own manga. Noah is studying at Wayne State University in Detroit and lives 20 minutes South in his hometown Wyandotte, Michigan. At Wayne, he studies Fine Arts and the Japanese language. Noah has a studio at the Downriver Council for the Arts, where he works with the Downriver Detroit Student Film Consortium. There, he is in charge of designing and illustrating a comic book, and helps grade students create short films. Noah has directed, shot, edited, written, and acted in short films, participated in the Wyandotte Street Art Fair, and has exhibited artwork at the Downriver Council for the Arts. In his free time, Noah practices martial arts, plays video games, enjoys music, reads manga, watches anime, and contemplates life– all of which influence his work.
